ABOUT OUR COMPANY

Create the dream home or office you desire with Akirbha Design Studio.

DESIGN STUDIO is an architectural design practice based in Vishakapatnam, with experience in high-end interior design along with residential and commercial infrastructure.This design studio combines the energy and enthusiasm of some budding architects and designers, with years of experience in understanding the challenges and providing sophisticated solutions through the language of design. AKIRBHA DESIGN STUDIO was established in 2015 by good friends and colleagues, Kiran Raju and Aditya Varma.
