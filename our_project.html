Our Projects

Residential

At Akirbha Design Studio, we specialize in creating exceptional residential spaces through our comprehensive architectural and interior design services. From conceptualization to execution, our talented team of architects and designers work closely with clients to bring their vision to life. With expertise in various residential typologies, we cater to apartments, villas, individual residences and more. We prioritize functionality and aesthetics in our residential designs, ensuring seamless flow and optimized space planning. Collaborating closely with clients, we curate personalized environments that reflect individual style and preferences. Our holistic approach integrates the indoor and outdoor spaces, creating a harmonious connection with the surrounding landscape. Whether you're seeking a complete renovation or starting from scratch, Akirbha Design Studio offers a tailored service that transforms houses into homes. Contact us today to discuss your residential project, and let us elevate your living experience with our expertise in architecture and interior design.

Office/Corporate

Akirbha Design Studio specializes in creating dynamic and functional office and corporate spaces. Our architectural and interior design services cater to the unique needs of businesses, creating environments that inspire productivity and reflect your brand identity. From conceptualization to execution, we collaborate closely with clients to design office spaces that optimize workflow and enhance employee well-being.

We integrate modern aesthetics with ergonomic considerations, creating spaces that strike the perfect balance between functionality and style. Our expertise extends to corporate interiors, where we curate professional environments that leave a lasting impression on clients and visitors. We focus on creating spaces that foster collaboration, innovation, and a positive work culture.

With a keen understanding of the importance of branding and identity, we incorporate your corporate values into the design, ensuring that the space aligns with your company's vision. At Akirbha Design Studio, we believe that an intelligently designed office environment can have a significant impact on productivity, employee satisfaction, and business success. Contact us today to discuss your office or corporate project, and let us transform your workspace into a vibrant and functional environment that embodies your company's ethos.

HOSPITALS

At Akirbha Design Studio, we specialize in creating innovative and patient-centric hospital environments. Our architectural and interior design services focus on optimizing functionality, promoting healing, and enhancing the overall patient experience. With a deep understanding of the unique requirements of healthcare spaces, we create designs that prioritize safety, efficiency, and comfort.

From reception areas to patient rooms, we carefully consider every aspect of the design to ensure a seamless flow and a calming atmosphere. Our expertise extends to specialized areas such as operating rooms, laboratories, and diagnostic facilities, where we integrate cutting-edge technology and design principles to create state-of-the-art healthcare environments. We recognize the importance of creating spaces that support the well-being of patients, their families, and healthcare professionals.

Our designs aim to foster a healing and nurturing environment that promotes a sense of trust, comfort, and confidence. Contact us today to discuss your hospital project, and let us collaborate with you to create a healthcare space that prioritizes patient care and enhances the overall healthcare experience.

Hotel/Restaurants

At Akirbha Design Studio, we specialize in creating captivating hotel and restaurant spaces that leave a lasting impression on guests. Our architectural and interior design services are tailored to enhance the guest experience and bring your vision to life.

With a keen eye for aesthetics and functionality, we design hotel and restaurant spaces that are both visually stunning and highly functional. From luxurious hotels to cozy restaurants, we carefully consider every detail to create spaces that evoke a unique ambiance and cater to the needs of your patrons.

Our expertise extends to optimizing the flow and layout of spaces, creating seamless transitions between different areas such as reception, dining, and lounges. We understand the importance of creating a welcoming atmosphere that entices guests to linger and enjoy their experience.
