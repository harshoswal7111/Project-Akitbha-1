Software-Courses

At Akirbha Design Studio, we offer specialized software tutoring for architecture and interior design Students and professionals. Our tutoring services are designed to enhance your skills and proficiency in industry-leading software, empowering you to excel in your field.

Our experienced tutors provide personalized training sessions tailored to your specific needs and level of expertise. Whether you're a student at a University seeking to learn the basics or an experienced professional looking to expand your knowledge, we have the resources and expertise to guide you and provide help with your assignments/work.

We cover a wide range of software commonly used in the architecture and interior design industry, including CAD softwares, 3D modeling tools, rendering softwares, and more. Our tutors are well-versed in the latest software advancements and techniques, ensuring that you stay up to date with the evolving technology landscape.

We believe that proficiency in software tools is essential for success in the modern design industry. Our tutoring services aim to equip you with the skills and knowledge necessary to efficiently and effectively bring your design ideas to life.

Contact us today to explore our software tutoring options and take your architecture and interior design skills to new heights. Let us empower you to unlock your full potential and achieve professional excellence.

AutoCAD

Learn the industry-standard software for 2D and 3D drafting and design. Master precise technical drawings, create accurate floor plans, and develop intricate architectural details using AutoCAD's comprehensive toolset.

Revit and Archid

Discover the power of Building Information Modeling (BIM) with Revit and Archicad. Develop comprehensive architectural and interior designs, collaborate with teams, and create accurate construction documentation using these industry-leading software solutions.

Enscape

Bring your designs to life with real-time visualization using Enscape and Lumion. Learn to create immersive, interactive, and photorealistic renderings, enabling clients and stakeholders to experience your designs before they are built.
