FREELANCING

In addition to our software tutoring services, Akirbha Design Studio also offers freelance software services tailored to meet our clients' specific requirements. Whether you need assistance with creating detailed drawings, developing 3D models, producing realistic renders, or creating engaging walkthroughs, our team of skilled professionals is here to help.

With our expertise in a range of software applications, including AutoCAD, SketchUp Pro, Rhinoceros 3D, Revit, Archicad, Enscape, Lumion, Vray, Photoshop, and Illustrator, we can provide comprehensive software solutions to bring your design ideas to life.

Our freelance services are designed to be flexible and customizable, allowing you to choose the specific software service you need for your project. We collaborate closely with you to understand your vision and requirements, ensuring that our deliverables meet your expectations and align with your project goals.

With our proficiency in these software tools, combined with our design expertise, we can assist you in creating high-quality drawings, precise 3D models, stunning visualizations, and immersive walkthroughs. Whether you are an individual designer, an architecture firm, or an interior design company, we are here to support you in delivering exceptional results to your clients.

Contact us today to discuss your specific software service requirements, and let us provide you with the professional assistance you need to bring your design projects to fruition.
